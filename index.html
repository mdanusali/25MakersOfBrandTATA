About the Book

25 Makers of Brand Tata is not just a book—it is a heartfelt tribute to the people and institutions that quietly, yet powerfully, built one of India’s most cherished legacies. It tells the story of Tata not merely as a business empire but as an idea—rooted in integrity, trust, and an enduring dedication to nation-building. It all began with the dream of Jamsetji Nusserwanji Tata—a dream far ahead of its time, shaped by courage and compassion. Generations nurtured and carried forward that dream, believing that success is meaningful only when it uplifts others. Moving beyond well-known figures, this book brings to light the contributions of administrators, labour leaders, and countless builders whose collective efforts powered Tata’s enduring success. Institutions such as Tata Steel, Tata Motors, Tata Consultancy Services, and Indian Institute of Science stand as pillars that transformed India’s industrial and technological landscape. At the heart of this legacy lies Jamshedpur—a city envisioned as a model of community, dignity, and progressive industrialisation. More than a chronicle of the past, this book serves as a reflection on the values that will continue to guide the Tata legacy into the future.
