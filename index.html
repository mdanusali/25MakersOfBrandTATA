About the Book

25 Makers of Brand TATA is more than a corporate story—it is a tribute to the people, values, and institutions that shaped one of India’s most trusted legacies. From Jamsetji Tata’s vision to the contributions of workers, engineers, and leaders, this book captures the human side of industry and nation-building.

Foreword by Dr. Ajoy Kumar

Former IPS officer and Member of Parliament, Dr. Ajoy Kumar reflects on Jamshedpur as a living example of Tata’s vision—where industry and humanity coexist. His foreword connects public service, governance, and the Tata ethos.

“Jamshedpur is not just a city—it is an idea.”

Introduction by Dr. Shakeb-ur-Rahman

As an academic and mentor, Dr. Shakeb-ur-Rahman provides a thoughtful perspective on the narrative—highlighting how this work goes beyond corporate history to capture the human spirit behind the Tata legacy.

About the Author

Md. Anus Ali, born and raised in Jamshedpur, brings a deeply personal perspective to this work. A program manager and PhD researcher, he combines lived experience with research to document the people and institutions behind Brand Tata.
